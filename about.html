We, are DEABnet.

A repository for machine learning, neural networking, and biometrics. We seek to change the way comsumers think about biometrics, in fact, we seek to make consumers want to think about biometrics.

DEABnet is, at first, our home for research and development. A place where we learn how to implement machine learning with neural networking while using biometric data as input. This will be built as a modular framework with which developers will build their own technologies on top of. That package, named KARMA, will then available for users to teach and then enjoy.

When our first stage is complete, DEABnet will host our learning framework as well as other technologies shared by developers. Ideally, biometric technology would be something users look for in their smartphones and accessories to take advantage of KARMA based services.

KARMA's purpose is to more widely spread the utilization of the information our body gives us to improve our experience with our favorite services. DEABnet's purpose is to make that all happen.
